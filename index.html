Methodology

We first met while covering the 2019 general elections in India for the BBC in New Delhi. After learning about our shared appreciation for art and design we started collaborating weekly to keep our creative juices flowing. We would write down random words on paper and pick two from the ever-growing bunch every week. We'd then create art pieces around the "Theme of the week" and showcase them to each other. As our contracts with the BBC ended and we moved on to different jobs this personal collaborative project also took on different forms. The latest is what you see on this web page.

Now instead of picking the theme from a set of chits, we have a python script do it for us. The code takes two words at random from a google sheet that we regularly update with words and phrases and creates a theme.
